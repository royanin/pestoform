What will I do with student feedback? Why Pestoform?

You address the feedback directly via email or in-person, or lead a classroom discussion the next time you meet. You might re-design your exams and assignments, or, you may decide to overhaul your course content when you teach the same topic again. Getting frequent student feedback allows the instructor to adjust the teaching process at numerous levels.

Pestoform is an online rendition of niche, pen-and-paper feedback gathering systems such as "minute paper" or "mud cards". As an online tool, it is always available, and saves your time by organizing the comments, thus helping you teach more efficiently. Pestoform is intuitive, and free!
