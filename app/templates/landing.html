How Pestoform helps better teaching and learning

Find out where your students get stuck • Motivate students to learn more by addressing their feedback • Be more inclusive by inviting anonymous feedback • Encourage meta-cognitive learning practices*

Pestoform is simple, well-organized, and free for educators

*Students learn better when they revisit a newly acquainted topic. The process of providing feedback and the followup discussions allow them to revise what they learned.
